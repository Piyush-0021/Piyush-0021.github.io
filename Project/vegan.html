7-Day Meal Plan

  1. Day 1: Tofu scramble, lentil soup, brown rice & veggie bowl
  2. Day 2: Overnight oats, hummus sandwich, tempeh stir-fry
  3. Day 3: Green smoothie, grain salad, black bean tacos
  4. Day 4: Oatmeal with flax, Buddha bowl, chickpea curry
  5. Day 5: Pancakes with maple, quinoa salad, vegetable stew
  6. Day 6: Smoothie bowl, veggie wrap, lentil bolognese
  7. Day 7: Avocado toast, roasted chickpeas, tofu fried rice

Suggested Supplements

  • Vitamin B12
  • Iron (if low levels)
  • Vitamin D3
  • Omega-3 (Algae-based DHA/EPA)

Tips for Success

  • Use variety: rotate legumes, grains, and veggies
  • Prepare in batches to save time
  • Use plant milk fortified with calcium & B12

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not eating enough protein
  • Skipping omega-3 sources
  • Depending on processed mock meats
