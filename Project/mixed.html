7-Day Meal Plan

  1. Day 1: Scrambled eggs with spinach, grilled chicken breast, brown rice
  2. Day 2: Smoothie with yogurt and banana, grilled salmon, quinoa salad
  3. Day 3: Oatmeal with eggs, roasted chicken, steamed vegetables
  4. Day 4: Toast with peanut butter, beef stir-fry with broccoli, sweet potatoes
  5. Day 5: Omelette with veggies, turkey sandwich, roasted veggies
  6. Day 6: Chia pudding with nuts, grilled fish tacos, kale salad
  7. Day 7: Greek yogurt with honey, steak with mashed potatoes, steamed asparagus

Suggested Supplements

  • Vitamin D3 (especially for those not getting enough sun)
  • Fish Oil (omega-3 fatty acids for heart health)
  • Magnesium (for muscle and nerve function)
  • Protein powder (if additional protein intake is needed)

Tips for Success

  • Include both animal and plant-based proteins to balance amino acids
  • Make sure to consume healthy fats, especially from fish and nuts
  • Eat fiber-rich vegetables and fruits for vitamins and antioxidants

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not getting enough variety in protein sources
  • Overconsumption of red meat (opt for lean cuts and fish)
  • Not eating enough vegetables with each meal
