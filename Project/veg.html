7-Day Meal Plan

  1. Day 1: Panner, vegetable salad, lentil soup
  2. Day 2: Greek yogurt with oats, chickpea curry with rice
  3. Day 3: Avocado toast with eggs, quinoa salad with feta cheese
  4. Day 4: Oatmeal with nuts, paneer tikka with roti
  5. Day 5: Smoothie with banana and spinach, veggie stir-fry with tofu
  6. Day 6: Whole wheat pancakes with honey, vegetable lasagna
  7. Day 7: Chia pudding with berries, mixed vegetable curry

Suggested Supplements

  • Vitamin B12 (if dairy is not sufficient)
  • Iron (if low levels)
  • Vitamin D3 (especially in winters)
  • Omega-3 (from algae-based sources)

Tips for Success

  • Incorporate dairy and eggs to maintain protein intake
  • Eat a variety of plant-based foods to ensure all nutrient needs
  • Include a good amount of fiber-rich vegetables

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not getting enough protein from plant-based sources
  • Not balancing the fats (relying too much on refined oils)
  • Overloading on carbs without enough vegetables
